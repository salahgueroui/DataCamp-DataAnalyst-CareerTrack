## Exercise
## Dependent vs. Independent events

## Instructions
## - Match each scenario to the correct category.
## - Classify each event as:
##   Dependent or Independent.

## MY SOLUTION:

## Dependent
## - The probability of a sports team winning a championship,
##   given they are currently at the top of the league.
## - The probability of being offered a job,
##   having made it to the final interview stage.
## - The probability of more than 100mm of rainfall,
##   given it is currently July.

## Independent
## - The probability of winning the lottery.
## - The probability of flipping a coin and getting heads,
##   given today is Tuesday.
## - The probability of rolling a six within two attempts
##   using a fair dice.
