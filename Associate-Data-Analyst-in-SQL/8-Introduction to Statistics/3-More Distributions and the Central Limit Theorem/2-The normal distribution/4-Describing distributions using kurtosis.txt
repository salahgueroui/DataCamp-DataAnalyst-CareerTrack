## Exercise
## Describing distributions using kurtosis

## Instructions
## - Select the definition that accurately represents
##   a normal distribution with negative kurtosis.
## - Choose one answer.

## Given Information
## - Kurtosis describes the size of the central peak
##   and how spread out the tails are.
## - Negative kurtosis refers to a distribution
##   that differs from a typical normal distribution
##   in peak height and tail thickness.

## Possible Answers
## - A distribution with the same kurtosis as a normal distribution.
## - A distribution with a larger central peak and smaller tails
##   compared to a typical normal distribution.
## - A distribution with a smaller peak and wider tails
##   compared to a typical normal distribution.

## MY SOLUTION:

## - A distribution with a smaller peak and wider tails
##   compared to a typical normal distribution.
