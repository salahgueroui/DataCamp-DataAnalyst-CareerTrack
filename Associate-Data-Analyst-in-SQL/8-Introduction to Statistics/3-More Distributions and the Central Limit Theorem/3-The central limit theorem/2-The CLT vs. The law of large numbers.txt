## Exercise
## The CLT vs. The law of large numbers

## Instructions
## - Classify each statement as belonging to:
##   The law of large numbers
##   or
##   The central limit theorem.
## - Match each statement to the correct concept.

## Given Information
## - A reminder plot shows distributions of 100 samples
##   and 1000 samples of a die roll.
## - The task is to distinguish between:
##   • Convergence of the sample mean
##   • The shape of the sampling distribution

## MY SOLUTION:

## The law of large numbers
## - This concept does not always result in a sample taking
##   the form of a normal distribution as sample size increases.
## - As the sample increases, the sample mean gets closer
##   to the value of the population mean.

## The central limit theorem
## - As the number of sample summary statistics calculated increases,
##   the distribution will more closely resemble a normal distribution.
## - This concept generally applies once the sample size is 30 or more.
