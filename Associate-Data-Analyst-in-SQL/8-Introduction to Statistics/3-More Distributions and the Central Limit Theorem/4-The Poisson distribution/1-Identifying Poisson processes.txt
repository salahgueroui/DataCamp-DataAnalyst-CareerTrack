## Given Information
## - The Poisson distribution models the number of times
##   an event occurs within a fixed interval of time or space.
## - It is typically used for:
##   • Counting events  
##   • Independent occurrences  
##   • Events happening at a constant average rate  

## MY SOLUTION:

## - Number of books sold per week.
## - Volume of mortgage applications received per month.

## Other distribution
